Privacy Policy for GeoFill: World Quiz

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

GeoFill: World Quiz ("the App") is a free geography quiz game developed by LappaProductions. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No data is shared with third parties

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data only on your device using local storage:

  • Your best times and high scores for each game mode
  • Your selected interface language

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Links

The App contains a link to Ko-fi (ko-fi.com) for optional donations. Tapping this link opens your device's browser and is subject to Ko-fi's own privacy policy. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for Ko-fi's content or privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted at the URL where this policy is hosted.
